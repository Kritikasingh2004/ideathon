WHAT IS IDEATHON?

IDEATHON is a unique platform by E-Cell FoET, UoL, to foster entrepreneurship and nurture the leaders and problem solvers of today and tomorrow. This event will provide a dynamic exchange of ideas, insights, and knowledge through workshops, speeches, and networking sessions. Attendees will showcase their innovative ideas, develop startup models, and pitch to potential investors in a supportive and collaborative environment.

Through IDEATHON, E-cell is inspired to provide students with an opportunity to showcase their innovative startup ideas, develop startup models, and compete with others to discover their true value. The event is designed to help students become more inventive and polished in their approach. Additionally, IDEATHON offers a platform for new startup models to pitch their ideas to potential investors.
